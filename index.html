Hi there! My name is Bryndon Humphrey. I am a self-taught Developer and Graphic Designer with extensive experience in project management, relationship management, and live events. I have a passion for automation, simplifying processes, and providing intuitive interfaces!

In my prior role as a Project Manager at AVT productions I spearheaded our initiative to refine processes. Our old processes typically involved creating and constantly updating a new spreadsheet every time there was a new metric to be tracked, which was tedious and took away from time that could be handling client deliverables. I was able to convert all the separate spreadsheets into custom Salesforce objects and create concise dashboards/reports to quickly pull up metrics in our weekly manager meetings. An additional flaw in our old spreadsheet process was that during busy seasons the data in the spreadsheets would often go un-updated. I was able to create a daily dashboard for all project managers to use which would show us, at a glance, which fields needed updating and which tasks needed to be touched that day. I took on that project to begin with because my ambitions are to bring simplicity and elegance to projects that are especially complex. It's a passion of mine to rethink things at try to make them more intuitive.

I'd appreciate the opportunity to chat more about your needs and see where I can bring value to your team. It would be a pleasure to take my learnings from three years of working in the fast-paced industry of live events and apply them to any challenges you may be facing. One key that I have picked up on is that whether in person or online, every event has a flow and understanding that flow is key to creating immersive experiences. My resourcefulness, project management expertise, and strong communication skills will make sure building your dreams into realities is an enjoyable and rewarding experience! Wherever your needs reside, I’d be a great teammate to aid in accomplishing your goals.
